# 🇳🇴 Noruega (Norway)

**Status:** 💡 Ideas *(✅ Visited / 🔄 Planning / 💡 Ideas)*

---

## 🌍 Overview
Norway is the land of dramatic fjords, steep mountains, and the midnight sun. It offers world-class hiking and some of the most scenic drives in the world.

---

## 📅 Trip Planning (Seasonal Guide)

| Season | Vibe | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|------|
| **Jun - Aug** | 🌞 Verano | Long days (Midnight Sun), all hiking trails open. | Crowded in hotspots (Geiranger). |
| **Sep - Oct** | 🍂 Autumn | Beautiful colors, fewer crowds. | Rainier and colder. |
| **Mar - Apr** | ❄️ Spring | **Skiing & Northern Lights.** Good snow in mountains. | Many hiking trails still snow-covered. |

> **⚠️ March 28 - April 5 Note:** Perfect for "Ski-and-Sail" or exploring the fjords with a snowy backdrop. The Northern Lights are still possible in the North (Tromsø).

## 🗺️ 7-Day "Fjord & Relax" Strategy
1. **Days 1-2: Bergen.** Explore the Hanseatic wharf and local seafood.
2. **Days 3-5: Flåm & Aurland.** Take the famous Flåm Railway. Stay at a fjord-side boutique hotel (e.g., 29|2 Aurland).
3. **Days 6-8: Loen.** Experience the Loen Skylift and relax in a high-end spa hotel (e.g., Hotel Alexandra).

---

## 🤿 Scuba Diving
- **Gulen Dive Resort:** Famous for world-class wreck diving and nudibranchs.
- **Note:** Dry suit is mandatory. Cold water diving expertise recommended.
