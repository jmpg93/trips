# 🇮🇸 Islandia (Iceland)

**Status:** 💡 Ideas *(✅ Visited / 🔄 Planning / 💡 Ideas)*

---

## 🌍 Overview
Iceland is the land of fire and ice. It offers some of the most dramatic landscapes on Earth, from massive glaciers and black sand beaches to active volcanoes and therapeutic hot springs.

---

## 📅 Trip Planning (Seasonal Guide)

| Season | Vibe | Pros | Cons | Recommendation |
|--------|------|------|------|----------------|
| **Jun - Aug** | 🌞 Verano | 24h light, F-roads open, green landscapes. | Crowded, expensive, no northern lights. | Ideal for Camper/Caravan. |
| **Sep - Oct** | 🍂 Autumn | Balanced light, Northern Lights start, cheaper. | Unpredictable weather starts. | Best overall balance. |
| **Mar** | ❄️ Late Winter | Dramatic ice, Northern Lights, ice caves. | Cold, wind, road closures possible. | 4x4 + Hotels (No Camper). |
| **Nov - Feb** | 🌨️ Deep Winter | Pure wilderness, ice caves. | Very dark, harsh driving conditions. | Hardcore adventure only. |
