# 🏔️ Zermatt (Ski Trip)

**Status:** 💡 Ideas *(✅ Visited / 🔄 Planning / 💡 Ideas)*

---

## 🌍 Overview
Zermatt is the pinnacle of luxury skiing. Car-free village, the iconic Matterhorn, and access to both Swiss and Italian slopes (Cervinia).

---

## 📅 Trip Planning (Spring Skiing)

| Feature | Condition in March/April |
|---------|-------------------------|
| **Snow Quality** | Excellent at high altitude (up to 3,883m). |
| **Weather** | Sunnier days, great for "Après-ski" on terraces. |
| **Crowds** | Busy during Easter (Semana Santa). Book ahead. |

## 🚆 Logistics (The Swiss Travel)
- **Geneva/Zurich to Zermatt:** ~3.5 hours by train.
- **Tip:** Buy the **Swiss Travel Pass** or **SBB Half Fare Card** for significant savings on the train and mountain lifts.

## 🗺️ The "Zermatt Experience"
1. **Skiing:** Cross the border to Italy for lunch in **Cervinia** (Pasta is cheaper/great).
2. **Matterhorn Glacier Paradise:** The highest cable car station in Europe.
3. **Gornergrat Railway:** Best views of the Matterhorn for a non-ski day.
4. **Relax:** Spend an afternoon in a luxury spa (e.g., Mont Cervin Palace or The Omnia).
