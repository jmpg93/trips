# 🇹🇿 Tanzania & Zanzibar

**Status:** 💡 Ideas *(✅ Visited / 🔄 Planning / 💡 Ideas)*

---

## 🌍 Overview
Tanzania offers the ultimate "Nature + Relax" combo: world-class Safaris in the Serengeti and Ngorongoro, followed by the turquoise waters and white sands of Zanzibar.

---

## 📅 Trip Planning (Seasonal Guide)

| Season | Vibe | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|------|
| **Jun - Oct** | ☀️ Dry Season | Best for Safari (animals gather at water). Clear skies in Zanzibar. | Peak prices and crowds. |
| **Nov - Feb** | 🌡️ Summer | Great for diving (high visibility). Calving season in Serengeti. | Hot and humid. |
| **Mar - May** | 🌧️ Long Rains | **Lowest prices.** Lush green landscapes. Fewest tourists. | **Wet season.** Some camps close. High humidity. |

> **⚠️ March 28 - April 5 Note:** You are entering the "Masika" (Long Rains). Expect afternoon showers. It's a gamble, but the "Emerald" landscape is beautiful and luxury lodges are significantly cheaper.

## 🗺️ The "Balance" Strategy (Safari + All-Inclusive)
1. **Days 1-4: The Wild (Nature).** Fly into Kilimanjaro (JRO). 3-day private Safari in Ngorongoro Crater and Tarangire (cheaper/closer than Serengeti).
2. **Days 5-8: The Blue (Relax).** Short flight to Zanzibar. Stay in **Nungwi or Kendwa** (the best beaches for swimming and diving as they are less affected by tides).

---

## 🤿 Scuba Diving
- **Mnemba Atoll:** Famous for dolphins and clear reefs.
- **Leander Shoal:** For more advanced divers.
- **Visibility:** May be reduced during the rains, but water remains warm (27°C).
